13th Hour Gaming

13TH Hour started off as a group of friends playing under the clan "Spawn Trapping" in Call of Duty: Ghosts. The team started turning heads and gaining attention from other clans in the scene. By the time CoD: Advanced Warfare released, the best players joined another group of talented players to form "Oddly Vicious" who went on to be number 3 in the world for clan wars. Once BO3 released, they took the best hardcore players from AW and slimmed down the active clan roster. It was then in 2015 that the original clan leader, AKADisaster, established the organization 13TH Hour Gaming.

In early 2017, Mr. Astro decided that it would be best to branch out to more eSports instead of sticking to solely to Call Of Duty, and with this decision, IIIX picked up a Counter-Strike: Global Offensive roster. The first era of 13TH Hour CS:GO played in CEVO main and season 27 of ESEA Open. After making it to ESEA intermediate, half of the roster decided to leave for personal reasons leaving Gibby & Munchz, the two Canadian players, to find a new roster. The canadians decided to stay local and merged with some Ottawa talent, a university team called the CarletonU ravens. The Ravens were local talent who've started to gather attention in the Ottawa & university scene, consisting of Mimic, Frosty, and Muffinatorz. In the beginning of season 28 of ESEA, Munchz was enlisted in the Canadian Military and had to leave the roster to prepare for his career. The team then picked up a local In game leader, FeaR to help give them proper direction. 13TH Hour CS:GO is very quickly making a name for themselves in the competitive CS:GO scene.

The organization has since gathered a large following on Twitter of 900+ followers and is making a name for itself in the competitve CoD and CS:GO scene.
